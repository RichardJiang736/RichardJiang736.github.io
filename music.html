Musical Analysis

Beyond individual reviews, I explore the theoretical frameworks and mathematical principles that underlie musical expression, examining how structure and emotion intersect in sound.

Theoretical Frameworks

Harmonic Analysis

Deconstructing chord progressions and harmonic relationships to understand how composers create tension, resolution, and emotional impact through harmonic language.

Rhythmic Patterns

Exploring the mathematical relationships in rhythm and meter, from simple time signatures to complex polyrhythmic structures found in world music traditions.

Melodic Construction

Analyzing how melodies are built using scales, intervals, and motivic development, creating memorable musical lines that resonate with listeners.

Cross-Cultural Perspectives

Eastern Musical Philosophy

Examining how Eastern musical traditions approach composition and performance, focusing on concepts like ma (negative space) and the importance of silence in music.

African Rhythmic Complexity

Investigating the sophisticated rhythmic systems found in African music and their influence on contemporary genres from jazz to electronic dance music.

Global Fusion

Analyzing how modern musicians blend elements from different cultural traditions to create new forms of musical expression that transcend geographical boundaries.
